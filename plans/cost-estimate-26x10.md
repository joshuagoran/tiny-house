# Material Cost Estimate — 26' × 10' Tiny House

**Draft — April 2026**
Excludes labor, permits, tools. Porch estimated separately at bottom.

## Surface Area Reference (26' × 10')

- Exterior wall area: 2 × (26' × ~10') + 2 × (10' × ~10') = ~720 sq ft
- Floor area: 26' × 10' = 260 sq ft
- Roof area: ~26' × 11' (with overhang) = ~286 sq ft
- Interior wall/ceiling area: ~900 sq ft (walls + ceiling finish)

## Notes

- Prices are 2025/2026 estimates. Lumber and material costs fluctuate.
- Local mill sourcing (hemlock siding, interior paneling, beams, counters) could save significantly vs retail.
- Reclaimed windows could cut the windows budget by 50%+.
- Gutex exterior insulation is the single most expensive insulation component — rigid mineral wool is a cheaper alternative.
- Clivus Multrum toilet pricing is a rough estimate — confirm US availability.
- Solar system is the B-lite starter config. Adding 4 more batteries later would be ~$2,700–3,200 additional.
